ABOUT US

what do we do ?

A2Z Tech Solutions is a pioneering company at the forefront of innovation of education and corporate ICT solutions. With a passion for technology and a commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a leader in this rapidly evolving industry. Our core specialization lies in providing comprehensive solutions in the supply, delivery, installation, training and maintenance of ICT solutions. A2Z Tech Solutions was founded by Loyiso Skweyiya, our current Managing Director since our commencement in the year 2020 Our business strategy is two fold : ICT distribution and innovative Solutions, i.e Research and Development (R&D) business. With this approach we are able to be an ICT value added solutions partner. As a customer-focused organization , we offer a full range of services to meet the needs of our clients.

From the initial stages of supply and delivery to seamless installation of custom hardware and software, cloud computing, DevOps support, and professional services. We ensure a hassle-free experience for our customers. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing thorough training to users, enabling them to harness the full potential of our systems effectively. This is comprehensive approach allows us to cater to a diverse clientele , comprising of both public and private sectors.
